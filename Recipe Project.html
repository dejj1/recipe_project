My Favorite Meal

The name of my favorite meal is Jollof Rice.



ORIGIN OF THE JOLLOF RICE

Jollof is believed to have originated in the Senegambia region of West Africa among the Wolof People, where it is known as benachin.



INGREDIENTS



S/N Ingredients Measurements
1 Vegetable Oil ⅓ cup
2 Tin of tomatoes 400g
3 Large red bell peppers 6
4 Medium-sized onions 3
5 Curry powder 2 teaspoons
6 Dried Thyme 1 teaspoon
7 Dried bay leaves 2
8 Chicken/beef stock 5 to 6 cups


DIRECTIONS

  1. In a blender, combine tomatoes, red bell peppers, chopped onions with 2 cups of stock.
  2. In a large pan, heat oil and the sliced onions. Season with a pinch of salt.
  3. Add 4 cups of the stock to the cooked tomato sauce and bring it to boil for 1-2 minutes
  4. Ready to serve
